Dissertation 畢業論文

Dynamic Option Hedging Strategy Via Reinforcement Learning 基於強化學習的動態選擇權避險策略

My final year dissertation exploring the application of reinforcement learning algorithms to develop dynamic option hedging strategies. This research combines financial theory with machine learning techniques to create adaptive hedging approaches that can respond to market changes in real-time. Completed as part of my undergraduate studies at University of Nottingham. 我的畢業論文探討如何運用強化學習演算法來開發動態選擇權避險策略。這項研究結合金融理論與機器學習技術， 創造出能夠即時回應市場變化的自適應避險方法。此論文為我在諾丁漢大學本科學習期間完成。
